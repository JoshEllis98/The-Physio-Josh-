My Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy services tailored to your specific needs, delivered in the comfort of your home.

What I Offer

Specialist Treatment Areas

I provide expert care across three core areas, each backed by clinical evidence and years of hands-on experience.

Musculoskeletal & Sports Injuries

Thorough assessment and targeted treatment for joint pain, muscle injuries, and sports-related conditions — with a special interest in ankle, knee and shoulder rehabilitation. Combining manual therapy with progressive exercise rehabilitation.

  • Back and neck pain
  • Shoulder, elbow & rotator cuff injuries
  • Knee ligament & meniscal injuries
  • Ankle sprains & Achilles tendinopathy
  • Muscle strains, tears & overuse injuries
  • Chronic pain management

Elderly Care & Falls Prevention

Helping older adults maintain independence through strength, balance, and mobility programmes — drawing on my experience delivering NHS hip & knee osteoarthritis classes. Gentle, progressive, and designed to fit into daily routines.

  • Balance and stability training
  • Strength & conditioning for older adults
  • Falls risk assessment & prevention
  • Mobility & walking aid assessment
  • Osteoarthritis management
  • Home environment safety assessment
Book Now →

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Structured, evidence-based rehab following surgery. Working closely with your surgical team's guidelines and adapting to your individual progress and goals.

  • Hip & knee replacement rehab
  • ACL reconstruction recovery
  • Rotator cuff repair rehab
  • Spinal surgery rehabilitation
  • Fracture & post-cast recovery
  • Return-to-sport programmes

Your First Visit

What to Expect

Here's what a typical first session looks like.

Thorough Assessment

I'll take a detailed history, discuss your goals, and carry out a comprehensive physical assessment to understand exactly what's going on.

Clear Diagnosis

I'll explain my findings in plain language — what the problem is, what's causing it, and what we can do about it. No jargon, no confusion.

Treatment Plan

Together we'll agree on a treatment plan with clear goals, expected timelines, and a home exercise programme tailored to your needs and environment.
