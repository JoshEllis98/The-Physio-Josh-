Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are the answers to the most common things people ask about home-based physiotherapy.

General Questions

Practical Details

Payment & Booking

I offer a free 15-minute discovery call so we can chat before you commit. An initial assessment (60 minutes) is £90 and follow-up sessions (45 minutes) are £75. Virtual appointments (30 minutes) are available for £50. I also offer discounted packages — a 4-session package at £268 (15% off) and a 6-session package at £372 (20% off). All in-person prices include travel within my core service area. Full pricing details are available on my Services page.
I accept bank transfer, card payment, and cash. Payment is due at the end of each session, or in advance for block bookings. I provide receipts for all payments, which can be used for insurance claims.
Yes, I'm recognised by most major health insurance providers. You'll typically need a referral from your GP or consultant, and an authorisation code from your insurer. I can provide all the documentation you need to claim back your fees. Please check with your specific provider for their requirements.
I understand that things come up. I ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may be charged at the full session rate. This helps me manage my schedule and offer availability to other patients who may be waiting.
Absolutely. I hold a BSc (Hons) in Physiotherapy, am registered with the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC), and am a member of the Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP). I carry full professional indemnity and public liability insurance, and have an enhanced DBS check. You can verify my registration on the HCPC register.
You can book through my Contact page using the online form and calendar, by phone, or by email. I aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours and can usually arrange an appointment within a few days.
